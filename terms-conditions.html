3. Booking & Reservation Policy

3.1 Booking Process

To book our services, you must:

  • Fill out the booking form with accurate and complete information
  • Receive and confirm a detailed quotation from our team
  • Pay the required advance payment or deposit as per our payment schedule
  • Receive a booking confirmation via email or phone

3.2 Advance Booking Requirements

We recommend booking at least:

  • Standard Events (Birthdays, Anniversaries): 7-14 days in advance
  • Weddings & Large Events: 30+ days in advance
  • Corporate Events: 15-20 days in advance
  • Same-Day Bookings: Subject to availability; additional charges of 20-30% may apply

3.3 Venue Access & Requirements

You must ensure that:

  • We have proper access to the venue for setup and decoration
  • The venue has adequate space for our team and equipment
  • You have the authority to use the venue
  • All restrictions, permissions, and special requirements are communicated at booking
  • Venue contact person is available on the day of the event

Additional charges may apply for:

  • Venues located more than 15 km from our location (Quthbullapur)
  • Difficult-to-access or multi-level venues
  • Extended setup time or overtime services
Important: Confirming your booking implies that you have the authority to use the venue and that all participants agree to our services being provided. You are responsible for obtaining all necessary permissions and ensuring safe access for our team.

5. Cancellation & Refund Policy

5.1 Customer Cancellation Policy

  • Day of event or no-show: 0% refund; full amount payable
