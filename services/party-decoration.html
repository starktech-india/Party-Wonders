Frequently Asked Questions

How much advance notice do you need for party decorations?

We recommend booking at least 7-10 days in advance for regular parties. However, for rush bookings, we can accommodate with 2-3 days notice depending on availability and the complexity of your design.

Do you provide customized themes?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating custom themes tailored to your specific preferences. Share your ideas, and our creative team will design and execute a unique decoration that reflects your vision perfectly.

Can you decorate outdoor venues?

Yes, we specialize in both indoor and outdoor decorations. For outdoor venues, we use weather-resistant materials and take weather conditions into account to ensure your decorations last throughout your event.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 7 days before the event are fully refundable. Cancellations within 7 days will incur a 50% service charge. We understand that plans can change, and we're flexible where possible.
