Frequently Asked Questions

Can I customize gift hampers?

Absolutely! We offer fully customizable hampers. Choose items, packaging, and design to create the perfect gift for your needs. Bulk corporate orders are also welcome with special pricing.

What's included in corporate event packages?

Our corporate packages include venue decoration, stage setup, lighting, branding integration, seating arrangements, and on-site coordination. We can customize packages based on your event size and requirements.

How far in advance should I book?

For large corporate events, we recommend booking 2-4 weeks in advance. Gift hampers and smaller events can be arranged with 3-5 days notice. Emergency bookings are accepted based on availability.

Do you offer same-day delivery for gifts?

Yes! We offer same-day delivery for gift hampers and smaller gifts in Hyderabad. Place your order before 12 noon to ensure same-day delivery. Rush delivery charges may apply.

Can you handle bulk corporate orders?

Absolutely! We specialize in bulk orders for corporate clients. We offer competitive pricing, consistent quality, and flexible customization. Contact our corporate team for special packages and quotes.

What if I need to make changes to my order?

We're happy to accommodate changes! For gifts, we can modify your order up to 24 hours before delivery. For events, changes are accepted with at least 48 hours notice.
