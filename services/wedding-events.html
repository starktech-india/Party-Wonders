Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book your services?

We recommend booking 3-6 months in advance for optimal availability and planning. However, we also accommodate last-minute bookings based on our team's capacity. Contact us to discuss your timeline.

Do you offer packages or customize based on budget?

Absolutely! We offer flexible packages for different budgets and can customize services based on your specific needs. Our team works with you to deliver maximum impact within your budget constraints.

What if I want changes on the day of the event?

Our team is trained to handle last-minute changes. We're flexible and accommodating to ensure your vision comes to life. Just communicate with our on-site coordinator immediately.

Do you handle setup and takedown?

Yes! Complete setup and professional takedown are included in all our wedding and event packages. We ensure your venue is perfectly prepared and restored after the celebration.

Can you work with external vendors or venues?

Of course! We have excellent relationships with multiple venues, caterers, photographers, and other vendors. We coordinate seamlessly with all external vendors to ensure a cohesive event.

What makes your designs unique?

Our designs blend timeless elegance with contemporary trends. Each design is custom-created to reflect your unique story, preferences, and personality. We don't do cookie-cutter designs!
